What is ideaFlash

产品介绍

ideaFlash is an AI-native product designed around personal context. Your notes, ideas, learning records and thoughts gradually build a personal context library that evolves over time.

ideaFlash 是一款围绕个性化 context 构建的 AI 原生产品。 用户的笔记、灵感、学习记录和思考会逐渐沉淀为一个不断成长的个性化 context 库。

Personal Context

个性化 Context

Each user owns a personal context library built from notes, interactions and knowledge. This context continuously evolves through filtering, learning and reinforcement.

每个用户都会拥有一个属于自己的 context 库。 这些内容来自笔记、用户与 AI 的交互以及长期积累的知识，并通过强化学习不断优化。

AI Avatar

AI 分身

Your AI avatar is your digital twin. It understands your background, preferences and long-term goals, and can help you complete digital tasks.

AI 分身是用户在数字世界的第二个自己。 它能够理解用户的背景、偏好和目标，并帮助完成各种数字世界的工作。
