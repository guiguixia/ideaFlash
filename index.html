IdeaFlash

Turn every fleeting idea into reality.

Product Vision

IdeaFlash is an AI-native platform designed to capture personal knowledge, build unique user context, and create a personal AI agent capable of working on behalf of the user.

Over time, IdeaFlash builds a unique context for each user, enabling seamless interaction with different AI assistants and delivering truly personalized experiences.

The long-term vision is to empower individuals to create their own AI agents and eventually operate a “one-person company” powered by AI.

Core Value Proposition

  • Capture and organize personal knowledge and ideas
  • Build long-term personalized AI context
  • Create a personal AI agent that understands the user
  • Enable collaboration between multiple AI agents
  • Help individuals transform ideas into real projects

Product Roadmap

The product evolves through several stages:

  • Notes – Capture personal knowledge and ideas
  • AI Agent (Chat) – AI that understands your context
  • AI Agent (Work) – AI capable of completing tasks
  • Multi-Agent Collaboration – AI agents collaborate across users
  • Content & Ecosystem – Distribution and discovery of AI-created work

Technology

  • Personalized context construction
  • Context engineering
  • Agent-to-Agent interaction (A2A)
  • Personal AI agents
